# 🌿 YIGA Health — Know Before It's Too Late

An AI-powered Sexual & Reproductive Health companion for Rwandan youth aged 15–30.

## What It Does

YIGA Health (YIGA = "Learn" in Kinyarwanda) gives young people a safe, anonymous space to ask sexual and reproductive health questions — with real answers in Kinyarwanda or English — and connects them to real clinics nearby.

### Three Core Features

- **💬 Anonymous AI Chat** — Ask anything about contraception, HIV, pregnancy, menstrual health, or relationships. Powered by Claude AI. No login, no data saved.
- **🏥 Clinic Locator** — Find youth-friendly health centers across Rwanda, filtered by service type (HIV testing, family planning, menstrual health).
- **📚 Health Education Cards** — Bite-sized guides on HIV & PrEP, contraception, menstrual health, and consent.

## Disclaimer

YIGA Health provides health information only — not medical diagnosis. Users with urgent concerns are encouraged to visit a qualified healthcare provider.
